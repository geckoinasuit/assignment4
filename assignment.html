Geckos: Why They're Great



Geckos are a varied and widespread subspecies of lizards. There are plenty of different types of geckos, many suitable for reptile enthusiasts and also for beginners as their first introduction to owning reptiles.


If you want to learn more about reptiles and owning reptiles, a great place to find information is Pangea Reptile. They are experienced breeders and their forums allow reptile owners across the world to communicate.
